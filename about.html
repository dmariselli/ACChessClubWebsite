About Chess Club

The Amherst College Chess Club has been around for a long time, but has only recently been resurrected under new management!

We are a fairly casual club and accept players of all skill levels. Even if you don't want to play chess, feel free to come by just to meet new people and enjoy a meal together.

If you are interested please contact Alex Chou at (achou17@amherst.edu)
